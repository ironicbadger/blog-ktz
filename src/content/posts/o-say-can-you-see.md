And so begins a new adventure, this time a big one. The biggest of 'em all... The USA.

Starting tomorrow, the next 3 weeks ought to be pretty special. We begin by flying to LA, driving up the Pacific Coast highway to San Francisco and Napa followed by a tour across Yosemite, Death Valley and Utah down to the Grand Canyon and Vegas finally ending up in Vancouver.

We're almost packed, still a few batteries charging though.

The trip gets off to a great start tomorrow with my first experience of an A380, let's just hope British Airways IT system holds up!
