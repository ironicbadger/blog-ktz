Let's see what 2025 brings but 2024 was alright.

-   Jan - Work trip to Phoenix
-   Cheeky solo day trip to the Grand Canyon via Sedona (stunning)
-   Kinda fell in love with Sonoran Desert fauna (Saguros!)
